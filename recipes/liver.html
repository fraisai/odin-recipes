Liver Cleansing Foods

Description

According to Dr. Eric Berg, symptoms of an underperforming or unhealthy liver include the following symptoms:

Symptoms

  • Itchiness
  • Jaundice
  • Lethargy
  • Aching or sore joints
  • Nausea
  • Lowered cognitive function and brain fog
  • Gallstones

List of Liver Cleansing Foods

The following is a list of foods that Dr. Eric Berg claims has the ability to "cleanse" your liver and improve the health of your liver.

  1. Cruciferous vegetables
  2. Garlic
  3. Turmeric
  4. Beets
  5. Mushrooms
  6. Avocadoes
  7. Extra virgin olive oil
